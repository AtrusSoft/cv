Tim Miller

Tim Miller is an American guitarist and composer at the forefront of progressive fusion and jazz.

He first became known for his distinctive acoustic-electric sound, melodic compositions, and unique approach to improvisation. His latest album, Synergy, is the culmination of this aesthetic vision. His other notable recordings include his Trio series.
